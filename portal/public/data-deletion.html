Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: April 22, 2026

You can delete any data that PILK has collected about you — OAuth tokens, connected-account profile data, and any content you had PILK process or store. There are three ways to do it.

Option 1 — Disconnect in the PILK dashboard (fastest)

  1. Sign in to your PILK dashboard.
  2. Open Settings → Accounts.
  3. Click Disconnect next to the account you want to remove.

Disconnecting immediately deletes the OAuth token and the profile snapshot PILK had stored for that account. Any content previously read from the account and saved to PILK's internal memory remains until you use Option 3.

Option 2 — Revoke access at the provider

If you can't reach the dashboard, you can cut PILK off directly from the service. The token PILK holds will stop working within minutes.

  1. Meta (Facebook / Instagram): facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools → find PILK → Remove.
  2. Google: myaccount.google.com/permissions → find PILK → Remove access.
  3. Slack: your workspace's Apps settings → find PILK → Remove.
  4. LinkedIn: LinkedIn Permitted services → find PILK → Remove.
  5. X (Twitter): Settings → Security and account access → Apps and sessions → Connected apps → find PILK → Revoke access.

Option 3 — Request full deletion

For full deletion — including any internal memory, logs, or stored content PILK may hold about you — email us:

Email aaron@skyway.media

Include the email address, phone number, or account handle(s) associated with your connected accounts so we can match and delete the right records.

Response time: we process every verified deletion request within 30 days and reply to confirm once it's done.
